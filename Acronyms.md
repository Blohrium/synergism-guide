## FAQ

```
Q: What is the difference between s[num] and sing[num]?
A: They mean the same thing — both refer to a singularity number. s[num] is the standard shorthand used throughout the guide.
```

```
Q: What does e[x]x[y] mean?
A: Exalt number [x], completion [y]. e1x1 is the first completion of Exalt 1; e6x25 is the 25th completion of Exalt 6.
```

```
Q: What is the difference between yamb and ramb?
A: yamb (yellow ambrosia) is standard ambrosia unlocked at singularity. ramb (red ambrosia) is a second ambrosia type unlocked after e5x1.
```
