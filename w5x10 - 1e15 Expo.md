## Challenge 15

After completing c14 you unlock c15. c15 cannot be completed, but you gain buffs from the highest coin exponent reached inside it (visible under Wow! Cubes -> Challenge 15). Enter c15, wait a handful of seconds, then leave - the recorded exponent is based on coins when you leave, not the highest point of the run.

After your first c15 run, retry some ascension challenges, especially c14, since multiple c14 completions become easy after the first.

Do a c15 run every 1-2 score runs from here on.

Secret c15 strategy: turn off auto research, use an Obtainium Potion, then buy r1x21 and r2x11x9.


## Run Cycle

From this channel onward, follow this run cycle:

**Cube -> Obt/Off -> c15**

Spend most time on score runs. Cube and Obt/Off runs should be short (1-3 minutes). A score run should never exceed 24 hours.


## Platonic Cubes

You unlock Platonic Cubes and corresponding upgrades here. You will not yet have enough score to reach them meaningfully.


## Research and Tesseract Automation

Tesseract building automation unlocks around this channel. Activate it, set to Percentage mode at 30. Spend all Tess in buildings for now, raising the percentage later. Once active, Tesseract buildings are automatically allocated and purchased after every ascension, regardless of whether you completed c10.

**r8x25** is a crucial permanent research between you and w5x10. Requirements: at least 80b tributes, 100m gifts, and 750k benedictions.

Swap shop upgrades to Obtainium EX to speed this up and avoid sacrificing ants, since Obtainium gain depends on ant sac amount per r5x7 (wait: per w5x7).


## Platonics and Maximising Cubes

You will start gaining some Platonics, but no more than 10-20 per ascension second at this stage, so they do not matter yet.

To buy w5x10, maximise cube gain by lowering corruption to allow more challenge completions without losing too much score. Continue 1-2 hour ascensions to open cubes/hypercubes and improve ascension score.

Once you have 1e14 tributes, start buying w5x10: spend 50% cubes on w5x10 and 50% on tributes. Once you reach 2e16 tributes, spend all cubes on w5x10 until maxed. **w5x10 costs 5e15 cubes total.**


## w5x10 - Alpha

After maxing w5x10, main goals are opening 12 million Platonic cubes and buying Alpha. You can buy up to 100 levels of p1x1 and 50 levels of p1x2. Ignore p1x3 and p1x4 in this channel. Save Plats for Alpha once you hit the 12 million softcap.

### Polymath, Time Dilation, and Ascension Speed

The Polymath talisman gives an Ascension speed bonus depending on rarity. Target godlike rarity at level 360 (+32.5% ascension speed); extraordinary at level 180 also gives +30%.

Lower Time Dilation and/or Defunded Education until you can acquire Polymath from sacrificing ants. Buy it quickly, since the ascension time multiplier stacks in real time. If you cannot acquire your intended Polymath talisman within 10 minutes, lower Time Dilation further.

Continue adjusting Time Dilation in following sections to maximise score while maintaining a godlike Polymath talisman.


## Alpha - p2x1x10

Alpha allows 5 extra ascension challenge completions each, which you should complete immediately. Then enter c15 for extra exponent.

Buy the 6th rune, **Infinite Ascent**, which gives +0.2% Quarks and +1% cubes per level.

Prioritise maxing p1x1 and p1x2 before moving on.

### p1x3 and Hypercube Bottlenecks

Prioritise Platonics toward maxing p1x3 if not yet maxed. As score grows, Hypercubes scale slower than Platonics - Platonics scale at score^0.75 while Hypercubes scale at score^0.5. From here, start being aware of when to save Hypercubes for Platonic upgrades.

### Platonics Distribution

If you have not opened 12.1 million Platonics, do that first. Then buy at least 50 levels of p1x4, then split remaining Platonics evenly between p1x4 and p2x1.

### p2x1 and Viscosity

Maxing p2x1 is the goal of this section. Buy levels one at a time - each level provides a strong buff allowing more challenge completions and raising Viscosity.


## p2x1x10 - p3x1 - Beta

Max research with an offering run before opening 3D-6D cubes.

### p2x2, p2x3, and p2x4

Purchase order: p2x4 -> p2x3 -> p2x2, or p2x3 -> p2x4 -> p2x2. The difference is minimal since this section is short. After p2x2 you can raise Time Dilation as long as you maintain a godlike Polymath talisman.

### p3x1 and Inflation

p3x1 lets you bypass the Deflation corruption by mirroring particles to diamonds, allowing you to raise Deflation directly to maximum.

Be very aware of Hypercube amounts here, as most will be spent on Platonic upgrades. Raise the percentage saved in Auto Tesseract Buildings to around 70% to open some Tesseracts.

### Beta

Beta has lower priority than p3x1 due to its high cost. Ascend occasionally, open cube/tess, update spirits/blessings/IA rune, update c15 expo, and repeat. Keep sufficient Hypercubes for the upgrade.
