## Content

Runes are leveled using Offerings. The **Salvage** rune divides the cost of leveling all runes, blessings, and spirits.

> ⚠ unverified: exact Salvage cost reduction formula — not documented in available sources.

Higher rarity talismans grant free levels of their associated rune — see Talismans - Start Here.

### Rune Level Targets

Level runes roughly evenly by cost. Priority targets for this stage:

- **Speed** — level to 500 after first offering grind; then 560 after the next reincarnation loop.
- **Duplication** — level to 130 after first offering grind; then 160 after the next reincarnation loop.

### Offering Grind

~5 minutes of offering grinding (Diamond Buildings tab → 0s prestiges) is enough to hit Speed 500 / Duplication 130. Repeat after the next reincarnation loop for Speed 560 / Duplication 160.
