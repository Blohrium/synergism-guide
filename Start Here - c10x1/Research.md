## Content

Research is purchased with Obtainium. For the most part, buy whatever is cheapest — research order is not critical.

If research feels slow, use Obtainium Potions (`Shift+P` / Shop tab) to get a chunk of Obtainium. Do not spend many quarks on potions.

### Key Researches

- **r2x22–r2x25** — unlock Particle Upgrades.
- **r3x11 / r3x13** — Auto Obtainium; purchase early.
- **r5x19–r5x20** — often affordable from leftover Obtainium after early ascends.

### Research Notation

Researches are referenced as `r[row]x[column]`. They also have tags in their in-game descriptions.
