## Content

Blessings improve rune effectiveness. They are purchased with Offerings and are retained on ascension — unlike most resources, they do not reset.

Spend leftover Offerings into Blessings before ascending rather than leaving them unused.

### Leveling

> ⚠ unverified: exact cost formula — roughly follows a pattern of: increase by 1, then reach 10, then increase by 10s rather than reaching 100. Needs source verification.

Level Blessings roughly evenly by cost, similar to how runes are leveled.
