## Content

Talismans unlock at c9x1 and are found under the Runes tab. They are leveled using Fragments (the triangles on the left side of the screen). Early on, ant sacrifices provide fragments — do not spend fragments manually before ants are established.

### Leveling

- **x1** — upgrades talisman by 1 level.
- **To Rarity** — upgrades talisman until it reaches the next rarity threshold.
- **Max** — upgrades talisman as many times as current fragments allow.

Rarity thresholds grant free rune levels — higher rarity means more free levels of the talisman's associated rune. When a talisman reaches Mythic or higher, it gains a Signature in addition to its Inscription.

> ⚠ unverified: whether rarity thresholds occur every 30 levels or at specific breakpoints (e.g. 1, 440 as shown in the in-game chart) — needs verification.

Talisman costs scale roughly with when they are obtained. Check in-game to level one at a time if unsure.

Talismans cannot be respecced — older pins suggesting respec are outdated.
