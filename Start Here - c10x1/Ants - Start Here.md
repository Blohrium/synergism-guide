## Content

### Ant Sacrifice

Ant Sacrifice requires 1e40 Crumbs. Click the ant icon or press `S` to sacrifice.

**First sacrifice** — do immediately for the ant speed boost.

**Subsequent sacrifices** — sacrifice to gain:
- Immortal Elo
- Offerings / Obtainium
- Fragments (for Talisman leveling)
- Anthill Count

### Crumb Milestones

- **1e300 Crumbs** — unlocks the Preserved Ant talisman by purchasing the first level of Mortuus Est Formicidae.
- **1e315 Galactic Crumbs** — enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1.
