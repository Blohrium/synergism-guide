## Quark Mechanisms

Quarks are earned via three repeatable actions under Settings → Special Actions.

**Add** — solve an addition problem for Quarks based on export size. 1 charge per hour, cap 24.
**Daily** — Quarks based on progress. Once per day.
**Time** — click OK within 1,000ms of a random timestamp shown between 6–21 seconds. Resets every 15 minutes; multiplier builds smoothly to ×24 at 24 hours idle.

> ⚠ unverified: Time code window is listed as 2,500ms in some pins and 1,000ms in source — needs source verification.

Export your save regularly. If something goes wrong you can import and try again.

## Quark Shop

Unlock order matters — some purchases cannot be refunded.

On first unlock: one level each in **Instant Challenge**, **Auto Offering**, and **Auto Obtainium** (cannot refund). Spend remaining Quarks on **Offering/Obtainium gain**.

Do not buy the **Shop Talisman** this channel — it is too expensive to level for the benefit at this stage. One more Ant level is better.

Do not spend Quarks on potions this channel.

## Transcension

Prestige to 100 Diamonds, then follow Accelerator Boost thresholds.

  - 1,000 Diamonds → Accelerator Boost
- **Accelerator Boost (1,000 Diamonds)**
  - 4e8 Diamonds → 1e5 Diamond Upgrade, Diamond Buildings
  - Prestige without Accelerators
  - Diamond Buildings Tab → Crystal Upgrade 1
  - 1e12 Diamonds → 1e12 Generator Upgrade, Diamond Buildings
  - Prestige without Coin Upgrades
  - 1e14 Diamonds → Accelerator Boost
- **Accelerator Boost (1e14 Diamonds)**
  - 1e5 Diamonds → 1e5 Diamond Upgrade
  - Prestige without Multipliers
  - 1.3e16 Diamonds → 1e16 Generator Upgrade, 1e15 Diamond Upgrade, 1e15 Automation Shop, 1e15 Diamond Buildings
  - 1e20 Diamonds → 1e20 Generator Upgrade, Diamond Buildings
  - 1e26 Diamonds → 1e26 Generator Upgrade → Accelerator Boost
- **Accelerator Boost (1e26 Diamonds)**
  - 1e15 Diamonds → 1e15 Diamond Upgrades

  - 1e30 Diamonds → 1e30 Generator Upgrade, Diamond Upgrades, Diamond Buildings

  - 1e15 Diamonds → 1e15 Diamond Upgrades

- 1e100 Total Coins → **Transcend** for 1 Mythos → Mythos Building

## Reincarnation

After first Transcension, repeat Transcensions while hitting Mythos thresholds.

**Offering grind** — in the Diamond Buildings tab, Prestige repeatedly at 0s to generate Offerings fast. Takes ~5 minutes.
- First grind: Speed 500, Duplication 130
- Second grind (after ~1.5e13 Mythos loop): Speed 560, Duplication 160

> ⚠ unverified: rune level targets — verify against current source.

<details>
<summary>Mythos progression thresholds</summary>

- **~1.3e7 Mythos** → Mythos Upgrades, 1e6 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings
  - Challenges 1/0/2/0/0
  - Generator Achievements (get Generator Upgrade Row 1 items #2–5 within a Transcend)
  - Offering grind → Speed 560, Duplication 160
- **~1.5e13 Mythos** → 1e13 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings
  - Transcend without Accelerators or Boosts
  - Farm Transcend Counts using Auto Transcend
  - Challenges 2/1/2/0/0
- **~1e25 Mythos** → Mythos Buildings
  - Farm Transcend Counts until Mythos Upgrade 1x3 is maxed
  - Transcend without Multipliers
  - Challenges 4/3/3/1/1 → 5/3/3/1/1
- **~1e42 Mythos** → 1e42 Mythos Upgrade, Mythos Buildings
- **~1e65 Mythos** → 1e65 Mythos Upgrade, 1e60 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings
- **~1e130 Mythos** → 1e87 Mythos Upgrade, Mythos Buildings
  - Challenges 6/4/3/1/1
- **~1e165 Mythos** → 1e165 Mythos Upgrades, Mythos Buildings
  - Challenges 7/4/4/1/1

</details>

Challenge notation is c1/c2/c3/c4/c5 completion counts.

**Mythos Upgrade 1x4** — multiplies Mythos gain per Transcension (max ×1e6). Farm at ~1.5e13 Mythos via Auto Transcend.
**Mythos Upgrade 1x3** — multiplies coin production per Transcension (max ×1e30). Farm at ~1e25 Mythos.

> ⚠ unverified: upgrade max values and farming thresholds — verify in source.

**1e300 Mythos Shards** → Reincarnate for 1 Particle → Particle Building.

After Reincarnating, use your Obtainium Potion (`Shift+P` or Shop tab) for 7,200 Obtainium. Buy cheap green/red QOL researches first. Get r3x11 and r3x13 for Auto Obtainium.

> ⚠ unverified: Obtainium Potion yield of 7,200 — verify in source.

## Challenges

Do challenges when they are easy or quick. Check for completions right after a Transcend before starting a normal run.

**Retry Challenges** re-enters a challenge automatically after completion for continuous farming — not for retrying a failed run. **Instant Challenges** from the Quark Shop significantly improves this.

<details>
<summary>Challenge progression targets by stage</summary>

| Stage | Completions (c1/c2/c3/c4/c5) |
|---|---|
| Early reincarnation | 1/0/2/0/0 |
| Mid reincarnation | 2/1/2/0/0 |
| Late reincarnation | 4/3/3/1/1 → 5/3/3/1/1 |
| Pre-ascension | 6/4/3/1/1 → 7/4/4/1/1 |

</details>

Notable unlocks: c5 gives prestige count multiplier, c7 gives transcend count multiplier (always max for PTR grinding). **c9x1 unlocks Talismans.**

## Runes

Runes are leveled with Offerings. Level roughly evenly by cost.

The **Salvage** rune reduces leveling costs for all runes, blessings, and spirits.

> ⚠ unverified: Salvage cost reduction formula — not documented in available sources.

Priority this channel: Speed and Duplication. See offering grind targets under [Reincarnation](#reincarnation).

Higher rarity Talismans grant free levels of their associated rune — see [Talismans](#talismans).

## Talismans

Talismans unlock at c9x1 and are found under the Runes tab. Leveled using Fragments (triangles on the left side of the screen). Ant sacrifices provide Fragments early — do not spend Fragments manually before Ants are established.

Talismans cannot be respecced. Any pins suggesting respec are outdated.

Rarity thresholds grant free rune levels. At Mythic rarity, a talisman gains a **Signature** in addition to its **Inscription**.

> ⚠ unverified: whether rarity thresholds occur every 30 levels or at specific breakpoints — needs source verification.

## Blessings

Blessings improve rune effectiveness and are purchased with Offerings. They **do not reset on Ascension** — spend leftover Offerings into Blessings before ascending rather than leaving them unused. Level roughly evenly by cost.

## Research

Research is purchased with Obtainium. Buy whatever is cheapest — order is not critical.

Key researches this channel:
- **r3x11 / r3x13** — Auto Obtainium; get these early.
- **r2x22–r2x25** — unlock Particle Upgrades.
- **r5x19–r5x20** — often affordable from leftover Obtainium after early ascensions.

## Ants

Ant Sacrifice requires 1e40 Crumbs. Click the ant icon or press `S`.

**First sacrifice** — do immediately for the ant speed boost.

Subsequent sacrifices yield Immortal Elo, Offerings, Obtainium, Fragments, and Anthill Count.

Ant time accrues before you reach the Ants mechanic after ascending — if you need to AFK, ascending early to accumulate Ant time can be better than sitting at the end of your ascension.

> ⚠ unverified: Ant time accrual behavior before Ants unlock post-ascension — verify in source.

Key crumb milestones:
- **1e300 Crumbs** — purchase first level of Mortuus Est Formicidae to unlock the Preserved Ant Talisman.
- **1e315 Galactic Crumbs** — enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1.

> ⚠ unverified: 1e315 Galactic Crumbs threshold for c10x1 — verify in source.

## Ascension

Ascend as soon as possible. You mainly want the cubes. Delaying to farm more is generally not worth it.

Before ascending, spend extra Offerings into Blessings — they persist through Ascension.

**WoW! Pass v1** unlocks after your first Ascension and gives a bonus to 3D cubes. For the first few ascensions: respec Quarks from Ant Speed to WoW Pass 1 before ascending, then back to Ant Speed after.

> ⚠ unverified: WoW Pass v1 cube bonus magnitude and Quark respec recommendation — verify in source.

## c10x1 Completion

Reach **1e315 Galactic Crumbs** for enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1. Complete it and proceed to the next channel.
