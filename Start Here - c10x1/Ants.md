## Content

### Ant Sacrifice

Ant Sacrifice requires 1e40 Crumbs. Click the ant icon or use the hotkey (`S`) to sacrifice.

**First sacrifice** — do immediately for the ant speed boost.

**Subsequent sacrifices** — sacrifice when you want to gain:
- Immortal Elo
- Offerings / Obtainium
- Fragments (for Talisman leveling)
- Anthill Count

### Unlocking Ants

You need 1e315 Galactic Crumbs to get enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1. The **Preserved Ant** talisman unlocks at 1e300 Crumbs by purchasing the first level of Mortuus Est Formicidae.

### Post-Singularity Ant Usage

- **Half-mind** (s150) — locks anthill timer to 10×, making ant gains scale linearly rather than quadratically below ~2 seconds. Reduces the impact of the global speed debuff at higher singularities.
- **One-mind** (s159–s162+) — locks ascension speed to 10× and converts multiples into cube multipliers. Also enables entering c15 without waiting for 2 seconds of in-game ascension time.
- For reborn elo grinding, use a corruption loadout of `16/0/16/0/5/16/0/16` or a clean loadout, combined with an ambrosia loadout with RCM and an obt-focused loadout. AFK through post-AoAG.
- Stats affecting ant speed and reborn elo generation: cubes, tesseracts, hypercubes, and platonic cubes opened.
