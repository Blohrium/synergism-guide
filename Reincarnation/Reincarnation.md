  - Transcend without Accelerators or Boosts

## Exhaustive Content

### Offering Grind
The ~5 min Offering Grind is done via 0s Prestiges in the Diamond Buildings Tab. This rapidly generates Offerings to level Runes.
- First grind target: Speed 500, Duplication 130
- Second grind target: Speed 560, Duplication 160
> ⚠ unverified — confirm these Rune level targets are still optimal against current source.

### Mythos Upgrade 1x3 and 1x4
- Mythos Upgrade 1x4: Multiply Mythos gain on Transcend by 1.01 per Transcension (Max: 1e6x). Farmed at ~1.5e13 Mythos by using Auto Transcend.
- Mythos Upgrade 1x3: Multiply coin production by 1.01 per Transcension (Max: 1e30x). Farmed at ~1e25 Mythos.
Both upgrades require farming Transcend Counts — Auto Transcend handles this passively.
> ⚠ unverified — confirm upgrade descriptions and max values in source.

### Challenge Progression
Challenge notation is c1/c2/c3/c4/c5 completion counts:
- 1/0/0/0/0 → 1/0/2/0/0 at ~1.3e7 Mythos
- 2/1/2/0/0 at ~1.5e13 Mythos
- 4/3/3/1/1 → 5/3/3/1/1 at ~1e25 Mythos
- 6/4/3/1/1 at ~1e130 Mythos
- 7/4/4/1/1 at ~1e165 Mythos
> ⚠ unverified — confirm challenge completion targets and Mythos thresholds in source or community records.

### Generator Achievements
At ~1.3e7 Mythos, Generator Upgrade Row 1 items #2-5 should be obtained within a Transcend to trigger Generator Achievements.
> ⚠ unverified — confirm Generator Achievement unlock conditions in source.

### Obtainium Potion
Grants 7,200 Obtainium. Use on cheap researches — green/red QOL first. r3x11 and r3x13 unlock Auto Obtainium, prioritize these.
> ⚠ unverified — confirm Obtainium Potion yield of 7,200 and Auto Obtainium research IDs in source.

1e300 Mythos Shards → Reincarnate for 1 Particle → unlocks Particle Building.

## FAQ

### FAQ 1 — When do I Reincarnate?
```
Q: When do I Reincarnate?
A: At 1e300 Mythos Shards. This gives you 1 Particle which unlocks the Particle Building.
```

### FAQ 2 — What is the Offering Grind?
```
Q: What is the Offering Grind?
A: In the Diamond Buildings Tab, Prestige repeatedly at 0s to generate Offerings quickly.
Takes ~5 minutes. Used to level Runes to Speed 500, Duplication 130 early on.
```

### FAQ 3 — What does farming Transcend Counts mean?
```
Q: What does farming Transcend Counts mean?
A: Using Auto Transcend to repeatedly Transcend, building up a count that feeds
Mythos Upgrades 1x3 and 1x4 which multiply coin production and Mythos gain respectively.
```

### FAQ 4 — When do I use my Obtainium Potion?
```
Q: When do I use my Obtainium Potion?
A: After Reincarnating. Use Shift+P or the Shop Tab. Spend on cheap green/red QOL
researches first, and make sure to get r3x11 & r3x13 for Auto Obtainium.
```
