### e6 and Fast Pseudosing

#### General Tactics for e6

1. Ensure all auto buyers are configured correctly and ambrosia loadouts include the exalt stabiliser. You will likely need both no-Hyperflux and Hyperflux setups.
2. Set challenge timer to 0/0.1/0.1 with no challenge skip.
3. Force c10 in A0 and c11–c14 using `(s)0c` (sac is optional depending on strength). Due to v4.0 changes, you must exit c10 for the next ascension challenge to appear. Clicking `c` is generally better to stay active in c15.
4. Time shift+A/ascend by watching the top right to catch when auto challenge sweeps c10.
5. Routing order: ascend immediately after c10 → c15 → first corruption setup (vis1) → max plat row 1 → c15 → second corruption setup (vis3) → max to p3x4 → c15 → third corruption setup (vis6) → omega → c15 → final corruption setup (vis10) ↔ c15 (run c15 whenever you judge enough challenge hepts have been gained).
6. Disable r2 hepts except Abyss (enable Abyss ~around Omega). Re-enabling r2 hepts at specific points can help coin expo — suggested timings: enable on entering e6, disable when saving for Omega, re-enable once after p4x2, and once again after p4x4. Buy Orbs and Challenge occasionally, timed to coincide with swapping into c15 to realise gains immediately.
7. Swap to Hyperflux setup at roughly 10–20 p4x4. Check the Ambrosia Heater for the exact value, though precise timing is rarely achievable.
8. If close to an exalt SR threshold (~15–30 mil or ~50–80 mil ambs) and the optimal loadout excludes it: near the end of the run, keep the Hyperflux loadout, disable all auto buyers, continue ascending until the timer runs out and hept gains drop. Then swap to max SR loadout, buy Abyss, and attempt p4x5.

#### Squeezing the Margins

Only apply these if stats are genuinely marginal. Otherwise easier improvements should exist.

1. Disable c1–c4 for auto challenge — start from c3 or higher.
2. If you have done enough e6 runs to know when p3x4 arrives, enter c15 immediately and swap to vis6 loadout.
3. Micromanage hept forge: disable challenge when not challenging next, disable Abyss when p4x4 is maxed until enough Chronos hepts, then disable everything but Abyss.
4. Manually buy p4x4 with auto plat buyer disabled so p3x3 does not consume all hepts. Combine with exalt SR swapping for cost reduction, though this requires fast and accurate inputs.

#### Down to 15 Seconds (Pseudosing Routing)

Pseudosing targets the fastest possible singularity completion rather than the highest singularity. Prefer s270–s275 over s280+.

Key improvements over a standard e6 run:

1. **Force c10** — wait ~200 ms outside c10, enter and wait 200–300 ms for completions. Massively speeds up A0 and c11–c14.
2. **Disable slow challenges** — c1–c5 give little score and at most provide completions for 3D cube bank. Disable some or all of c1–c5 for speed. Disabling all c1–c5 impact is unclear.
3. **Limit corruption setups** — use 3 setups or 2: one covering r8x25 to Omega (vis1 or vis3), one vis6 from Omega to p4x2, and one vis10 after p4x2. Combine with auto ascend.
4. **c15 spam** — replaces alternating between hept runs and c15 runs. Run one hept run after Omega, then with auto ascend and c15 as the only active ascension challenge, the game will spam c15 automatically until AoAG and the resulting exponent gain.

User-made scripts for various stages of this process are available in the HyperSynergism thread.

### s281, e8 and Horseshoe

> ⚠ Completing e8x9 is currently intentionally impossible.

To buy AoAG in e8x1–e8x8: enter c14, disable all auto Offering spenders, and spam ant sac.

After e8x5, an expensive shop upgrade becomes available. It makes the Horseshoe rune reduce **all** singularity debuff effects (excluding salvage and ant elo debuff) by 0.1% per level and adds 3 bonus rune levels. With 100+ rune levels, this can reduce singularity difficulty by up to 27% — the cube gain, plat cost, and hept cost effects all stack for AoAG.

### Achievements and Grinding PTR Counts

The endgame of achievement grinding is reborn elo and PTR count grinding.

**Reborn elo** — use a clean corruption loadout or `16/0/16/0/5/16/0/16`. Use an ambrosia loadout with RCM and an obt-focused loadout. AFK through post-AoAG. Use the SLAE sheet in Re-Oct to estimate time to max.

Stats that affect ant speed and reborn elo generation: cube/tess/hyper/plat opened.

**PTR counts** — grind ant sac. Strategy is performance-dependent; no approach is clearly superior:
- Autochallenge times 0/0/0 — may or may not help
- Autoreincarnation time 0 — may or may not help
- Holding R hotkey — may or may not help
- Increasing key send rate — may or may not help
- JS script to reincarnate every tick — may or may not help

Always max c5 and c7 — they give prestige count and transcend count multipliers respectively.
