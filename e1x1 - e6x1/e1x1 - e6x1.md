## Content

### Ambrosia

Ambrosia bar fills at a set rate of bar points per second. Each full bar grants ambrosia equal to luck/100 (fractional luck gives a random chance at an extra one). Bar requirement grows linearly (with offset) to total ambrosia, and doubles twice per order of magnitude at 10^n and 3×10^n for every n starting at 4 (i.e. first doubling at 10k, second at 30k, etc.).

Buy the **Patreon bonus** first — it gives a large yamb bar speed buff, typically over +100%. Then buy the **10 tutorial module levels** for +50% cubes and +10% quarks at a cost of 100 ambrosia. Both apply regardless of loadout.

Because ambrosia is freely refundable, maintain multiple specialised loadouts:

- **Cube loadout** — switch to this before ascending. Use CM1 only; add LCHM1 and QCHM1 if you have excess ambrosia.
- **Quark loadout** — switch to this after a c15 expo update and during add code usage; open 1 Platonic to activate quark multipliers. Use QM1, CQHM1, and LQHM1.
- **Luck loadout** — your default loadout for the majority of time; maximises ambrosia generation. Use LM1, CLHM1, and QLHM1.

### Elevator and Lookahead

You have two singularity counts: **current** (shown in the top bar; determines penalties) and **highest** (shown on the perks page; determines perks and GQ/Exalt shop unlocks).

To advance singularity, buy the AoAG rune from p4x5. You can look ahead 1–5 sings depending on upgrades owned — **aetherflux** (GQ shop) and **discombobulator** (oct shop) each extend lookahead.

Looking ahead updates your current sing (penalties) to the target sing. Highest sing only ever increases by 1 per lookahead, and only if you were previously at your highest sing. Buying AoAG updates highest sing to current sing if that would increase it.

**Example:** At s100 with +3 lookahead, jumping to s103 sets current to s103 and highest to s101. Buying AoAG in s103 then sets highest to s103.

**Mechanics and tricks:**
- Export your save before any elevator use.
- You can decrease current sing with the elevator freely.
- Raising current sing with the elevator costs a singularity reset.
- To unlock any perk, beat the singularity below it and look ahead from there.
- Highest sing updates even while inside an exalt.
- Entering an exalt in a higher sing while at your highest sing with AoAG increases highest sing by +1, as if you looked ahead normally.
- Entering or exiting an exalt does not reset the singularity timer, but grants GQ for the sing just left.
- These three points allow pushing singularities while preserving the timer for DELTA and PHI upgrades, as long as you have an exalt available in a higher sing.

> ⚠ unverified: there are perks that scale with **current** singularity — most notably Even More Quarks (not labelled as such in-game) and skrauq (unlocks at s200), and the three ant perks: Bring to Life, Invigorated Spirits, and Advanced Cheating Tactics.

### Corruptions and Post-AoAG Gameplay

Post-AoAG corruption setups remain largely stable from p4x2: raise TD to cap and HC beyond 7, then 9, as you grow stronger — this gains obt for mythic Wow! talisman and more challenge completions. As challenge completions accumulate to the point of capping your hept setup, begin raising Vis beyond 10.

For octs after Cookies 4, `14/14/14/14/14/14/14/14` outperforms `10/14/14/14/14/14/14/14` for a while. Once l15 corruptions are unlocked, swap immediately to something like `10/15/15/15/15/15/15/15` for octs.

Raising Vis to 11 or 12 starts paying off around e2–e3. Each Vis level increases difficulty but also increases score significantly.

Overnight only octeracts and ambrosia. In this channel, prioritise octeracts overnight as they give the most immediate benefit. Ambrosia overnighting begins in the next channel.

### New Exalts

Complete the first clear of any exalt as soon as it unlocks — they range from easy to trivial at unlock.

**e2** — Maximum of one completion each from c1–c14 (the Not So Challenging perk bypasses this, adding 5 c8 completions). Use the highest corruption levels you can while still clearing c10. Raise Viscosity last. If you have Cookie 3 and Cx16, turn off auto-platonic upgrades and avoid Omega — Cx16 removes 2× cubes with minimal buff.

**e3** — Ascension count multiplier is locked to 1, with a limited ascension budget before debuffs begin (÷2 asc speed beyond maximum). You do not gain ascension count from ascending without c10 — turn off auto challenge and manually complete c10. c15 can be run without c10 to gain exponent without spending an ascension. Export saves frequently to correct mistakes.

**e4** — Same as e1 but Octeract upgrades are disabled. Slightly harder at A0 in later completions, otherwise similar.

**e5** — Same as e1 and e4 but easier; ambrosia loadouts are disabled. Complete e5x1 as soon as you reach s166 to unlock new upgrades and new ambrosia modules.

### Half-Mind and One-Mind

**Half-mind** unlocks at s150. It makes ant gains scale linearly rather than quadratically to time when the anthill timer is below ~2 seconds, locking the anthill timer to 10×. This reduces the global speed debuff impact at higher singularities.

**One-mind** unlocks around s159–s162+. Without it, you cannot enter challenges in c15 because the game requires 2 seconds of in-game ascension time to enter c15. One-mind locks ascension speed to 10× and converts multiples of it into cube multipliers. Effects: c15 is now accessible without waiting, cube/s becomes predictable, and octeract gains increase (ascension speed affects oct gain, raised to the 0.5, further increased by some oct upgrades).

## FAQ

```
Q: What is the difference between current and highest singularity?
A: Current sing determines penalties. Highest sing determines perks and GQ/Exalt shop unlocks. They can differ when using the elevator or lookahead.
```

```
Q: Can I lose highest singularity progress by using the elevator?
A: No. Highest sing can only increase, never decrease. Descending with the elevator only changes current sing.
```

```
Q: Why can't I enter c15?
A: Between s159 and s162+, entering c15 requires 2 seconds of in-game ascension time. One-mind fixes this by locking ascension speed to 10×.
```

```
Q: When should I raise Vis above 10?
A: Around e2–e3, once you are capping challenge completions in your hept setup. Each Vis level is harder but gives significantly more score.
```

```
Q: Should I overnight ambrosia or octeracts?
A: Octeracts in this channel. Ambrosia overnighting starts in the next channel (e6x1–s256).
```
