## FAQ

<details>
<summary>What is the difference between s[num] and sing[num]?</summary>

They mean the same thing. s[num] is the standard shorthand used throughout the guide.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What does e[x]x[y] mean?</summary>

Exalt number [x], completion [y]. e1x1 is the first completion of Exalt 1; e6x25 is the 25th completion of Exalt 6.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the difference between yamb and ramb?</summary>

yamb (yellow ambrosia) is standard ambrosia unlocked at singularity. ramb (red ambrosia) is a second ambrosia type unlocked after e5x1.

</details>
