## You have now completed your first Ascension. Now what?

You can see a new tab (Wow! Cubes), and you can spend your Wow! Cubes (3D cubes) in Cube Upgrades (Wow! Cubes > Cube Upgrades). After your first ascension, buy w1x1 which increases cube gains, and open the rest in Wow! Cubes > Cube Tributes. Opening cubes gives buffs that make the game faster. In future ascensions you can choose to open more cubes, buy automation upgrades (w1x2–w1x9), or buy stronger upgrades (w2x2).

You also unlock a new building type (Buildings > Tesseract Buildings). Tesseract Buildings produce constants that reduce taxes and unlock 10 new upgrades. Buy one of each building first, as this gives the most constant.

You can start overnighting Anthills after your second ascension — they persist through ascensions and boost Ant Speed. Auto Sacrifice requires your device to stay on.

You will get nothing from ascending unless you get a c10 completion.

## General Tips

- Play on synergism.cc or Steam — they give ~140% of the Quark buff from the Patreon buff, making the game significantly easier.
- Export your save often.
- There aren't many red herrings — you don't lose progress from buying upgrades.
- Check the Corruption Glossary, it is very helpful.

## Happy Hour Bell

If logged in with an account, the Happy Hour Bell gives +25% Quarks and +50% cubes of all types. You can wait in a long ascension and cash in during a bell for more cubes, and use your daily/add/time codes during this time for a small Quark buff.

## Automations

- **Auto P/T/R** — Stay off unless grinding PTR count overnight.
- **Auto Ascend** — Only useful from c11–c12. Useless otherwise.
- **Auto Anthill Sacrifice** — During long runs, set Mod to Time at 0 seconds and leave your device on. Reborn ELO mode is decent but sacrificing manually is better for min-maxing.
- **Auto Tess Buildings** — Turn on at Percentage mode, value 10–90 depending on whether you need Tess Gifts or Tess Buildings.
- **Auto Challenge** — See Challenge section below. Everything else unmentioned should stay on.

## Shop Upgrades

There are 2 sets of important shop upgrades for cube runs and score runs: Chronometer 1/2 and Season Passes. During an ascension swap to Chronometer upgrades to build ascension time, then at the end swap back to the corresponding cube upgrades to maximise cube gains. Invest 5–10% quarks into Wow Pass v1 for 3–4D cubes in most cases even if not aiming for it.

Other useful early upgrades: c10 requirement reducer, Reincarnation EX, red calculator (provides 1 real-life ascension minute per level per add code, add code regenerates every 3600s), blue calculator, plastic talisman.

### Assessment of Every Shop Upgrade

- **Instant Challenge Completions** — Buy immediately, best upgrade available.
- **Offering Auto/Obtainium Auto** — Buy 1 level immediately, then ignore until end of guide.
- **Offering EX/Obtainium EX/Ant ELO boost/Cash Grab** — Juggle in runs as wanted.
- **Plastic Talisman** — Buy immediately after getting Mythic Ant Talisman for the first time, or right before your first Ascension.
- **Season Passes** — Always respec to them before ascending in a cube/score run.
- **Season Pass** — Always buy in cube runs, spend 5–10% otherwise.
- **Season Pass 2** — Buy in score runs before 1e15 expo, or when saving for p4x2x1. Spend 5–10% otherwise.
- **Season Pass 3, Season Pass Y** — Distribute to maximise hept gain. Spend 70–90% on both except when saving for p4x2x1.
- **Reincarnation Challenge EX** — Buy when you reach the challenge cap, max immediately after Omega.
- **Challenge 10 Requirement Reduce** — Buy a few for easier c11/c12 completions. Max asap after Omega.
- **Quark-Enriched Wow! Cubes/Tesseracts/Hypercubes** — Buy after Omega, possibly sooner depending on how idle you are.
- **PL-AT calculator** — Buy quickly, especially first level, but not urgent.
- **PL-AT X calculator** — Never buy, not worth it until end of guide.
- **PL-AT Ω calculator** — Buy all 10 levels at once when you reach 1.2 million spendable quarks.
- **Chronometer 1/2** — Always respec to these while AFK in a cube/score run. Balance between 1 and 2 to maximise ascension speed.
- **Infinite Ascent** — Buy around p2x1x10 when you have over 100k quarks.
- **Improved Daily Code 1** — Never buy, not worth it until end of guide.
- **Constant EX** — Buy first level at 2 million spendable quarks. Never buy second level until end of guide.
- **Powder EX** — Buy until each level costs more than 0.2–0.5% of spendable quarks.
- **Quark Hepteract 1** — Never buy, not worth it until end of guide. 16k quark hept is more valuable than the first level of this.

## Offering Runs (post c12)

Occasionally do offering runs (no corruptions or campaigns) for off/obt. As you gain more stats you gain more ants and off/obt, allowing you to increase blessing/spirits/IA rune/achievement talisman/ascension speed ant. This also increases constant gains as offering runs have much higher global speed than regular cube runs.

## Challenge 11

Your next goal is c11. Ascensions should be as short as possible at this stage.

**c11 stats recommendation:** Midas talisman, 100k cube tributes, green cube upgrades. Active players can try up to 300k tributes and red upgrades. The attempt should take less than an hour.

Steps: Do short runs with Ant ELO boost occasionally → invest quarks in Obtainium EX → max research up to r5x23 → invest quarks in Offering EX → level up runes and all talismans to Mythic.

## Corruption, Campaigns, and Tesseract Gifts

After c11 you unlock corruptions and campaigns, which are major features throughout gameplay. You start with 2 corruptions: Viscosity and Drought. Corruption makes ascension harder but raises ascension score, which increases 3D/4D gains. Try all unlocked campaigns and compare gains. Tokens from campaigns give some buff but aren't the focus.

Tesseract Gifts unlock here — equivalent of Cube Tributes for Tesseracts. Don't focus too much on opening Tesseracts; buy one Tesseract building each instead. If opening Tesseracts, don't open more than 20k before c12 as you will hit a softcap.

c11–c14 each unlock 15 new research. Check their effects.

## Challenge 12

**c12 stats recommendation:** r6x15, c11x3, level ~80 blessings, one of each Tesseract buildings 1–3, 1m cube tributes, green cube upgrades. The attempt should take less than an hour.

Steps: Do short runs with Ant ELO boost occasionally → invest quarks in Obtainium EX → max research up to r5x23 → invest quarks in Offering EX → level up runes.

### Auto Challenge

r6x25 unlocks Auto Challenge, toggled with hotkey `C`. Set parameters to 0.1/0.2/0.2, use Auto Challenge a few times, then manually squeeze out a few challenge completions before ascending.

## Long Ascensions and New Corruptions

After c12 cubes grow linearly with time after 10 ascension seconds, and 2 new corruptions unlock with the corruption cap raised from 5 to 7. This allows much longer ascensions without losing cube gains. The new corruption unlocks new campaigns. Goal remains maximising cube/tess per second.

The score bonus from each corruption is multiplicative — distribute corruptions evenly to maximise score.

## Challenge 13

**Requirements:** ~120m tributes, 20k gifts, 5th Tesseract building, all cube upgrades up to w4x3 maxed, achievement level 80, r7x10. With these stats c13 takes about 2 minutes. Wait for 1e17 constant if you can't complete it quickly.

c13 has a specific strategy around challenges and taxes: turn off Retry Challenge and complete c6 for the tax buff, then c8 so ants function (unless you have w5x8), then c9 to gain fragments. Sacrifice, wait, sacrifice again, buy fragments if possible, then enter c10 directly.

## Hypercubes

c13 also unlocks 2 new corruptions: Defunded Education and Great Depression II. Try the new campaigns and maximise score gain.

You also unlock Hypercubes, which like Cubes and Tesseracts can be opened. Goal until c14 is to maximise Hypercube gain, continue opening everything, and complete ascension challenges after every score run.

## Challenge 14

**Requirements:** ~10k hypercubes opened, ~1m Tesseract gifts, ~4b cube tributes, r8x10, e26 constant. Only need to max row 3 cube upgrades and buy some row 4. Remember to sacrifice ants to gain fragments and upgrade talismans.
