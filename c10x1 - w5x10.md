## First Ascension

After your first Ascension you unlock the Wow! Cubes tab and Tesseract Buildings.

Buy **w1x1** first - it increases cube gains. Open the rest of your cubes in Wow! Cubes -> Cube Tributes. Opening cubes gives buffs that speed up the game. In future ascensions you can choose to open more cubes, buy automation upgrades (w1x2-w1x9), or buy stronger upgrades (w2x2).

Buy **one of each Tesseract Building** first. Tesseract Buildings produce constants that reduce taxes and unlock 10 new upgrades. Buying one of each gives the most constant.

You will get nothing from ascending unless you have a c10 completion.

After your second Ascension, Anthill Count persists through ascensions and you can start overnighting anthill grinds. Auto Sacrifice requires your device to stay on.


## General Tips

- Play on synergism.cc or Steam for roughly 140% of the Quark buff from the Patreon buff, making the game significantly easier.
- Export your save often.
- There are not many red herrings - buying upgrades generally does not cost you progress.
- Check the corruption loadouts in the [Corruptions](#corruptions) section before running.


## Happy Hour Bell

If logged in with a Synergism account, the Happy Hour Bell gives +25% Quarks and +50% cubes of all types. You can run a long ascension and cash in during a bell for more cubes, and use your daily/add/time codes during this time for a Quark boost.

## Quark Shop

During an ascension, swap to **Chronometer 1/2** upgrades to build ascension time, then at the end swap back to the corresponding cube upgrades to maximise cube gains. Invest 5-10% of Quarks into Wow Pass v1 for 3D-4D cubes in most cases even if not actively aiming for it.

## Offering Runs

After c12, occasionally do offering runs (no corruptions or campaigns) for off/obt. More ants and off/obt from these runs lets you increase blessings, spirits, IA rune, achievement talisman, and ascension speed ant. Offering runs also have much higher global speed than cube runs, which increases constant gains.


## Challenge 11

Your next goal is c11. Keep ascensions as short as possible at this stage.

Recommended stats: Midas talisman, 100k cube tributes, green cube upgrades. Active players can try up to 300k tributes and red upgrades. The attempt should take less than an hour.

Steps: short runs with Ant ELO boost occasionally -> invest Quarks in Obtainium EX -> max research up to r5x23 -> invest Quarks in Offering EX -> level all runes and talismans to Mythic.


## Corruptions

c11 unlocks corruptions and campaigns. You start with 2 corruptions: Viscosity and Drought. Corruptions make ascension harder but raise ascension score, which increases 3D/4D cube gains. The score bonus from each corruption is multiplicative - distribute corruptions evenly to maximise score.

Try all unlocked campaigns and compare gains. Tokens from campaigns give some buff but are not the main focus.

## Tesseract Gifts

Tesseract Gifts unlock after c11 - the equivalent of Cube Tributes for Tesseracts. Do not focus too much on opening Tesseracts yet; buy one Tesseract building each instead. If opening Tesseracts, do not open more than 20k before c12 as you will hit a softcap.

c11-c14 each unlock 15 new research rows. Check their effects when they unlock.


## Challenge 12

Recommended stats: r6x15, c11x3, level ~80 blessings, one of each Tesseract buildings 1-3, 1m cube tributes, green cube upgrades. The attempt should take less than an hour.

Steps: short runs with Ant ELO boost occasionally -> invest Quarks in Obtainium EX -> max research up to r5x23 -> invest Quarks in Offering EX -> level up runes.

r6x25 unlocks **Auto Challenge**, toggled with `C`. Set parameters to 0.1/0.2/0.2, use Auto Challenge a few times, then manually squeeze out a few more completions before ascending.

After c12, cube gains grow linearly with time after 10 ascension seconds, and 2 new corruptions unlock with the cap raised from 5 to 7. This allows much longer ascensions without losing cube gain rate. The new corruptions unlock new campaigns.


## Challenge 13

Requirements: ~120m tributes, 20k gifts, 5th Tesseract building, all cube upgrades up to w4x3 maxed, achievement level 80, r7x10. With these stats c13 takes about 2 minutes. Wait for 1e17 constant if you cannot complete it quickly.

c13 has a specific challenge order around taxes: turn off Retry Challenge, complete c6 for the tax buff, then c8 so ants function (unless you have w5x8), then c9 to gain fragments. Sacrifice, wait, sacrifice again, buy fragments if possible, then enter c10 directly.

c13 also unlocks 2 new corruptions: Defunded Education and Great Depression II. Try the new campaigns and maximise score gain.

After c13 you unlock Hypercubes. Goal until c14 is to maximise Hypercube gain, continue opening everything, and complete ascension challenges after every score run.


## Challenge 14

Requirements: ~10k hypercubes opened, ~1m Tesseract gifts, ~4b cube tributes, r8x10, e26 constant. Max row 3 cube upgrades and buy some row 4. Remember to sacrifice ants to gain fragments and upgrade talismans.

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>What do I do right after my first Ascension?</summary>

Buy w1x1 first, then open remaining cubes in Wow! Cubes -> Cube Tributes. Buy one of each Tesseract Building. Your next goal is c11.

</details>

<details>
<summary>When should I Ascend?</summary>

Before c12: as soon as you beat c10, unless going for a permanent research. After c12: you do not have to ascend immediately - longer runs do not lose cube rate, and are especially useful after c14.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are Campaigns?</summary>

A Corruptions tutorial with rewards. They guide you in the right direction generally, though they become a bit finnicky after c14.

</details>

<details>
<summary>I bought w1x10 but where are the tools to automate Ascensions?</summary>

On the Corruptions tab, which requires a c11 completion to see.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Why am I not getting fragments from sacrificing Ants?</summary>

You need a c9 completion for that.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Why did my Cube gain drop or increase out of nowhere?</summary>

Probably a Happy Hour Bell ending or starting. A cube decrease of roughly 33% or increase of roughly 50% out of nowhere is most likely caused by a bell.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Should I buy Platonic Upgrades / what do I do with Platonic Cubes?</summary>

Do not buy Platonic Upgrades here. Open all Platonic Cubes you get.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What corruptions should I use?</summary>

See the corruption loadouts in the Corruptions section above. Distribute corruptions evenly to maximise score - the bonus is multiplicative.

</details>

<details>
<summary>When should I do offering runs?</summary>

After c12. No corruptions or campaigns. Used to gain off/obt, improve blessings/spirits/IA rune, and increase constants.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Why can't I level Blessings or Spirits even though I have enough Offerings?</summary>

Check the red/yellow input box next to the level button. The default value is 0, which tells the game to buy 0 levels. Set it to 10 or higher.

</details>
