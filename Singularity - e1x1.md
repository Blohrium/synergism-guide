## First Singularity

Immediately after Singularising, buy **Starter Pack**, **Shop Bonanza**, and **Cookies 1** from the Golden Quark shop.

The new loop is completing singularities until s25, which unlocks e1x1.

Singularising loses all Quarks from the current run but gains Golden Quarks and unlocks new features. The best time to Singularise is whenever you feel like it - technically the most optimal time is ASAP every time, but Singularising requires a meaningful amount of active play to get going, so if you don't have an hour or more free, just farm Quarks instead and come back later.

You don't generally need to buy GQ upgrades at a specific Singularity count - it's fine to save up until you can afford them.


## Things Speed Up

A lot of guidance is written from the perspective of S0's slow pace, or the first few singularities after. Just like ascensions sped up after your first few, singularities speed up too.

As you accumulate permanent speed and cube multipliers (notably CubeZ and Asc Speed 3 in the Quark shop, unlocked from Shop Bonanza), progress accelerates. This makes finicky min-maxing (offering runs, manually balancing hept ratios, c15 researches, slow runs saved for one big ant sac) less worthwhile compared to just getting back to racking up or reinvesting gains.

Later, a loop like this works well (similar pre-Omega too, minus the separate hept/cube runs):

1. Idle in hept for a few minutes
2. Ascend to cube, spend hept run rewards - by the time you're done clicking around you've spent enough time in the cube run
3. Ascend to zero/uncorrupted, spend cube run rewards, upgrade IA rune/blessings/spirits (auto-rune handles this later)
4. While still zero, take a quick spin through any uncapped challenges (c15 if nothing else)
5. Back to hept, repeat

### Free Levels

Each daily code grants rolls for free GQ upgrade levels.

| Upgrade | Chance | Free Levels |
|---|---|---|
| GQ3 | 0.1% | 0.2 |
| GQ2 | 0.2% | 0.2 |
| GQ1 | 0.7% | 0.2 |
| C. Inferno / Obt. Tsunami / Off. Tempest | 0.5% each | 0.25 |
| C. Blaze | 5.5% | 0.5 |
| Obt. Flood / Off. Storm | 6.0% each | 0.5 |
| C. Flame / Obt. Wave / Off. Charge / Asc. Count | 20.0% each | 1.0 |

Effective level = bought + free, when free <= bought. When free > bought:

```
eff = bought + (free - bought)^0.5
```

A later upgrade changes this to `eff = (bought * free)^x` for some exponent x. Effective level is always the maximum of the two formulas, so the upgrade never has a negative effect.


## Campaign Corruption Tricks

Starting at Singularity 4, the **Automatic Campaigns** perk grants Challenge 10 completions on ascension - outside the campaign itself - if your current corruption is of equal or higher "difficulty" than a campaign.

This does not require every individual corruption to be equal or higher than the campaign's. You can craft a loadout that's easier to get c10 completions in while still scoring high enough on the campaign's overall Difficulty Score to count.

Example: Campaign L (#50) has a reference loadout of `13/13/13/13/13/13/13/13` (difficulty score 22,032). Once you've purchased Level 14 Corruptions for 1,000 GQ, a loadout of `6/14/14/14/14/14/14/12` (difficulty score 22,096) qualifies for the same campaign while giving far more c10 completions.

Other qualifying loadouts for campaign 50 include `0/14/14/14/14/14/14/14` (22,352) and `3/14/14/14/14/14/14/13` (22,064), though they may give fewer c10 completions than the `6/14/...` loadout. Try a few - results vary.


## When to Complete a Singularity

Only begin a new singularity if you have time to reach AoAG (Antiquities of Ant God). Otherwise, sitting in a singularity to accumulate Quarks, GQ, and cubes from daily resets and codes is more efficient.


## Octeracts

Octeracts unlock at s8+ (exact timing depends on when you buy the GQ upgrade). Octeract gain begins once ascension score exceeds 1e23. Early gains are small - only the first upgrade and some Obt/Off upgrades will be affordable. Do not focus heavily on Octeracts until the next channel.


## s25 and e1x1

At s25, enter e1x1 immediately. Completion takes roughly 4-12 hours. GQ upgrades are disabled in e1x1 - treat it as a normal singularity with fewer stats. After e1x1, complete e1x2 while stats are still fresh.

## Post-Singularity Runes

### Infinite Ascent (IA)

Boosted by TAEM and RCM ambrosia modules - include both in quark and cube loadouts post-e5x1 for more Quarks via IA rune levels.

### Antiquities of Ant God (AoAG)

Purchased from p4x5. Buying this rune advances your Singularity, and is the primary goal of each Singularity run.

### Horseshoe

Becomes relevant after the e8x5 shop upgrade. Each level reduces all Singularity debuff effects (excluding salvage and ant elo debuff) by 0.1%. The e8x5 upgrade grants 3 bonus Horseshoe levels. With 100+ rune levels, this can reduce Singularity difficulty by up to 27% - cube gain, plat cost, and hept cost effects stack for AoAG.


## Post-Singularity Talismans

### Wow! Square

Must be at least Mythic before running hepteract runs - below Mythic you miss a 20% boost.

### Talisman Buff 1

A GQ shop upgrade. Buy when cheap - low priority but provides a meaningful talisman boost over time.

### TAEM (Talisman Ambrosia Embedding Module)

An ambrosia module unlocked post-e5x1. Include in quark and cube loadouts - it increases your Infinite Ascent rune level, which gives more Quarks.


## Post-Singularity Challenges

### c15

c15 expo (best Challenge 15 exponent) is a key progression stat post-singularity. Run c15 with an Off loadout, ideally including an RCM module. After AoAG penalties are removed, c15 gives significantly more exponent, which gives more Quarks.

Occasionally check if a better c15 exponent is achievable pre-AoAG if within c6 - waiting a few seconds without auto-challenge can let the exponent rise slightly.

After AoAG, c15 spam becomes viable: with auto ascend on and c15 as the only active ascension challenge, the game spams c15 automatically, producing massive exponent after Omega.

### c10

Required for ascension count gain in e3. In e3, exit c10 manually after each completion to trigger the next ascension challenge - auto challenge will waste ascensions if left on.

### e2 Challenge Cap

In e2, a maximum of one completion each from c1-c14 is allowed (the Not So Challenging perk adds 5 c8 completions bypassing this). Use the highest corruption levels clearable while still completing c10, raising Viscosity last.


## Post-Singularity Ants

### Half-Mind (s150)

Locks the anthill timer to 10x, making ant gains scale linearly rather than quadratically below roughly 2 seconds of anthill timer. Reduces the global speed debuff impact at higher singularities.

### One-Mind (s159-s162+)

Locks ascension speed to 10x and converts multiples of ascension speed into cube multipliers. Two effects: c15 becomes accessible without waiting for 2 seconds of in-game ascension time, and Octeract gains increase (ascension speed affects Oct gain, raised to the 0.5, further increased by some Oct upgrades).

### Reborn Elo Grinding

Use a corruption loadout of `16/0/16/0/5/16/0/16` or a clean loadout. Use an ambrosia loadout with RCM and an obt-focused setup. AFK through post-AoAG. The SLAE sheet in Re-Oct can estimate time to max the achievement.

Stats affecting ant speed and reborn elo generation: cubes, tesseracts, hypercubes, and platonic cubes opened.

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>Do I need to finish every singularity quickly?</summary>

No. Sitting in a singularity and collecting Quarks/GQ/cubes from codes and daily resets is often better than rushing a new one you don't have time for.

</details>

<summary>When do I get Octeracts?</summary>

The GQ upgrade unlocks them at s8+. You need ascension score above 1e23 to start gaining them, and early gains are very low.

</details>

<summary>What do I do immediately after my first singularity?</summary>

Buy Starter Pack, Shop Bonanza, and Cookies 1 from the GQ shop. Then focus on Wow Pass Z and Ascension Speed 3.

</details>

They increase your Infinite Ascent rune level, which gives more Quarks. Include both in quark and cube loadouts from e5x1 onward.

</details>

</details>

Before hepteract runs. It needs to be at least Mythic or you miss a 20% boost.

</details>

Talisman Ambrosia Embedding Module - an ambrosia module that boosts your Infinite Ascent rune level for more Quarks. Include it in quark and cube loadouts from e5x1 onward.
