## Quark Mechanisms

Quarks are earned via three repeatable actions under Settings -> Special Actions.

**Add**
Solve an addition problem for Quarks based on export size. 1 charge per hour, cap 24.

**Daily**
Quarks based on progression. Once per day.

**Time**
Click OK within the window of a random timestamp shown between 6 and 21 seconds. Resets every 15 minutes; multiplier builds smoothly to x24 at 24 hours idle.

Export your save regularly. If something goes wrong you can import and try again.


## Quark Shop

On first unlock: one level each in **Instant Challenge**, **Auto Offering**, and **Auto Obtainium**. These cannot be refunded. Spend remaining Quarks on **Offering/Obtainium gain**.

Do not buy the **Shop Talisman** this channel. It is too expensive to level for the benefit at this stage. Do not spend Quarks on potions this channel either.

Prestige to 100 Diamonds, then follow the Accelerator Boost thresholds below.

"Prestige without X" steps trigger specific multiplier conditions. Do them when listed; they are quick.

## Reincarnation

After first Transcension, repeat Transcensions while hitting the Mythos thresholds below.

**Offering grind** - in the Diamond Buildings tab, Prestige repeatedly at 0s to generate Offerings fast. Takes about 5 minutes.

First grind targets: Speed 500, Duplication 130
Second grind targets (after the ~1.5e13 Mythos loop): Speed 560, Duplication 160

<details>
<summary>Mythos progression thresholds</summary>

**~1.3e7 Mythos** -> Mythos Upgrades, 1e6 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings
- Challenges 1/0/2/0/0
- Generator Achievements (get Generator Upgrade Row 1 items #2-5 within a Transcend)
- Offering grind -> Speed 560, Duplication 160

**~1.5e13 Mythos** -> 1e13 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings
- Transcend without Accelerators or Boosts
- Farm Transcend Counts using Auto Transcend
- Challenges 2/1/2/0/0

**~1e25 Mythos** -> Mythos Buildings
- Farm Transcend Counts until Mythos Upgrade 1x3 is maxed
- Transcend without Multipliers
- Challenges 4/3/3/1/1 -> 5/3/3/1/1

**~1e42 Mythos** -> 1e42 Mythos Upgrade, Mythos Buildings

**~1e65 Mythos** -> 1e65 Mythos Upgrade, 1e60 Mythos Automation Shop, Mythos Buildings

**~1e130 Mythos** -> 1e87 Mythos Upgrade, Mythos Buildings
- Challenges 6/4/3/1/1

**~1e165 Mythos** -> 1e165 Mythos Upgrades, Mythos Buildings
- Challenges 7/4/4/1/1

</details>

Challenge notation is c1/c2/c3/c4/c5 completion counts.

**Mythos Upgrade 1x4** multiplies Mythos gain per Transcension (max x1e6). Farm at ~1.5e13 Mythos via Auto Transcend.
**Mythos Upgrade 1x3** multiplies coin production per Transcension (max x1e30). Farm at ~1e25 Mythos.

At **1e300 Mythos Shards**, Reincarnate for 1 Particle, which unlocks the Particle Building.

After Reincarnating, use your Obtainium Potion (Shift+P or Shop tab) for 7,200 Obtainium. Buy cheap green/red QOL researches first. Get r3x11 and r3x13 for Auto Obtainium.


## Challenges

Do challenges when they are easy or quick. Check for completions right after a Transcend before starting a normal run.

**Retry Challenges** re-enters a challenge automatically after completion for continuous farming. It is not for retrying a failed run - if you fail, get more stats first. **Instant Challenges** from the Quark Shop significantly improves this.

<details>

|---|---|

Notable: c5 gives a prestige count multiplier, c7 gives a transcend count multiplier - always max these for PTR grinding. **c9x1 unlocks Talismans.**


## Runes

Runes are leveled with Offerings. Level roughly evenly by cost.

The **Salvage** rune reduces leveling costs for all runes, blessings, and spirits.

Priority this channel: Speed and Duplication. See offering grind targets under [Reincarnation](#reincarnation).

Higher rarity Talismans grant free levels of their associated rune. See [Talismans](#talismans).


## Talismans

Talismans unlock at c9x1 and are found under the Runes tab. Leveled using Fragments (the triangles on the left side of the screen). Ant sacrifices provide Fragments early - do not spend Fragments manually before Ants are established.

Talismans cannot be respecced. Any pins suggesting respec are outdated.

Rarity thresholds grant free rune levels. At Mythic rarity, a talisman gains a Signature in addition to its Inscription.

## Blessings

Blessings improve rune effectiveness and are purchased with Offerings. They do not reset on Ascension - spend leftover Offerings into Blessings before ascending rather than leaving them unused. Level roughly evenly by cost.


## Research

Research is purchased with Obtainium. Buy whatever is cheapest - order is not critical.

Key researches this channel:

r3x11 / r3x13 - Auto Obtainium; get these early.
r2x22-r2x25 - unlock Particle Upgrades.
r5x19-r5x20 - often affordable from leftover Obtainium after early ascensions.


## Ants

Ant Sacrifice requires 1e40 Crumbs. Click the ant icon or press `S`. Do your first sacrifice immediately for the ant speed boost.

Subsequent sacrifices yield Immortal Elo, Offerings, Obtainium, Fragments, and Anthill Count.

Ant time accrues before you reach the Ants mechanic after ascending. If you need to AFK, ascending early to accumulate Ant time can be better than sitting at the end of your ascension.

Key crumb milestones:

1e300 Crumbs - purchase first level of Mortuus Est Formicidae to unlock the Preserved Ant Talisman.
1e315 Galactic Crumbs - enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1.


## Ascension

Ascend as soon as possible. You mainly want the cubes. Before ascending, spend extra Offerings into Blessings - they persist through Ascension.

**WoW! Pass v1** unlocks after your first Ascension and gives a bonus to 3D cubes. For the first few ascensions: respec Quarks from Ant Speed to WoW Pass 1 before ascending, then back to Ant Speed after.


## c10x1 Completion

Reach 1e315 Galactic Crumbs for enough Coin Multiplier to complete c10x1, then proceed to the next channel.
