### First Singularity

Immediately after singling, buy **Starter Pack**, **Shop Bonanza**, and **Cookies 1** from the Golden Quark shop.

The new loop is completing singularities until s25, which unlocks e1x1.

### Permanent Shop Upgrade Order

#### Shop Bonanza

- **Wow Pass Z** — buy at least 100 levels at the end of s1 alongside Ascension Speed 3. Buy at the end of most singularities when not expanding qhept.
- **Ascension Speed 3** — buy at the end of every singularity unless spending on something else.
- **Red Book** — buy when cheap; low priority.
- **Qhept Expo 2** — too expensive early; buy post-liquidation.
- **PL-AT δ Calculator** — same as above.
- **Daily Code 2** — buy when cheap; do not prioritise.
- **Instant Challenge 2** — strong QoL; removes ascension challenge resets. Buy whenever the QoL is worth it to you.

#### Shop Liquidation Sale (s9+)

- **Powder Conversion 2 (Insta Powder)** — buy 1 level immediately. Grants powder on orb purchase at midnight's conversion rate, eliminating the need to wait for midnight. Then buy when you can go 1→10 and 10→100.
- **Ascension Speed 4** — buy some levels; gets expensive fast. Prefer WowZ and Asc Speed 3 if not maxed.
- **Cyan Calculator** — buy when cheap.
- **Qhept Expo 3** — buy when cheap; mostly a next-channel upgrade.
- **Daily Code 3** — buy when cheap; low priority.
- **Ambrosia Speed / Ambrosia Luck** — buy in the next channel.
- **Wow Pass ._(lost)** — buy in the next channel when farming octs for l15 corruptions.
- **Ex Off and Obt / Cash Grab 2** — buy only when very cheap and no better options exist.
- **Cube to Quark Corner Gain** — buy when cheap; strong quark multiplier on cube opens.

### GQ Upgrade Priorities

- Any **pack** — buy immediately at the start of the required singularity.
- **Shop Bonanza** — buy immediately.
- **GQ1** — very strong; increases GQ gain directly. Can be bought alongside Cube Flame/Blaze/Inferno.
- **GQ2 / GQ3** — buy when cheap; otherwise ignore. GQ3 is worth it if taking a break.
- **Cookie Recipes 1–3** — buy immediately when available. Cookie Recipes 4 is generally affordable in e1x1–e5x1; Cookie Recipes 5 in e5x1–s256.
- **Cube Flame / Blaze / Inferno** — prioritise over Obt/Off GQ upgrades. Most impactful during singularities; buy when not saving for major upgrades.
- **Off / Obt (all 3 tiers)** — spend roughly under 10% of GQ here; helps counter penalties.
- **Improved Ascension Gain** — same priority as Obt/Off; buy opportunistically.
- **Level 14 Corruptions** — buy immediately.
- **Level 15 Corruptions** — buy when possible; adds 1 free corruption level and meaningfully increases cube gain.
- **Potion Decanter** — fine to buy; do not prioritise over better options.
- **Octeracts** — buy after Shop Liquidation if short on GQ; otherwise buy immediately.
- **Shop Liquidation** — buy instantly. Massive QoL and long-term boost.
- **Talisman Buff 1** — buy when cheap.
- **Bigger Challenge Caps** — buy instantly; more score means more cubes.
- **Campaign Token Gain** — buy when cheap.
- **Platonic Sells Out** — buy when possible; not a current focus as most oct upgrades are inaccessible.
- **I Wish My Quark Hepteract Was Marginally Better** — buy only when cheap; very expensive.

### Free Levels

Each daily code grants N rolls for free GQ upgrade levels. Probabilities per roll:

| Upgrade | Chance | Free Levels |
|---|---|---|
| GQ3 | 0.1% | 0.2 |
| GQ2 | 0.2% | 0.2 |
| GQ1 | 0.7% | 0.2 |
| C. Inferno / Obt. Tsunami / Off. Tempest | 0.5% each | 0.25 |
| C. Blaze | 5.5% | 0.5 |
| Obt. Flood / Off. Storm | 6.0% each | 0.5 |
| C. Flame / Obt. Wave / Off. Charge / Asc. Count | 20.0% each | 1.0 |

Effective level = bought + free when free ≤ bought. When free > bought: `eff = bought + (free - bought)^0.5`. A later upgrade changes the formula to `eff = (bought * free)^(something)`; effective level is always the maximum of the two formulas, so the upgrade has no negative effect.

### When to Complete a Singularity

Only begin a new singularity if you have time to reach AoAG. Otherwise, sitting in a singularity to accumulate quarks, GQ, and cubes from daily resets and codes is more efficient.

### Octeracts

Octeracts unlock at s8+ (exact timing depends on when you buy the GQ upgrade). Octeract gain begins when ascension score exceeds 1e23. Early gains are small; only the first upgrade and some Obt/Off upgrades will be affordable. Do not focus on octeracts heavily until the next channel.

### s25 and e1x1

At s25, enter e1x1 immediately. Completion takes roughly 4–12 hours. GQ upgrades are disabled in e1x1 — treat it as a normal singularity with fewer stats. After e1x1, complete e1x2 while stats are still fresh.

## FAQ

```
Q: Do I need to finish every singularity quickly?
A: No. Sitting in a singularity and collecting quarks/GQ/cubes from codes and daily resets is often better than rushing a new one you don't have time for.
```

```
Q: When do I get Octeracts?
A: The GQ upgrade unlocks them at s8+. You need ascension score above 1e23 to start gaining them, and early gains are very low.
```

```
Q: What do I do immediately after my first singularity?
A: Buy Starter Pack, Shop Bonanza, and Cookies 1 from the GQ shop. Then focus on Wow Pass Z and Ascension Speed 3.
```
