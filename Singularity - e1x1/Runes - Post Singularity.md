## Content

### Infinite Ascent (IA)

Boosted by TAEM and RCM ambrosia modules — include both in quark and cube loadouts post-e5x1 for more quarks via IA rune levels.

### Antiquities of Ant God (AoAG)

Purchased from p4x5. Buying this rune advances your singularity. The primary goal of each singularity run.

### Horseshoe

Becomes relevant after the e8x5 shop upgrade. Each level of Horseshoe reduces all singularity debuff effects (excluding salvage and ant elo debuff) by 0.1%. The e8x5 upgrade also grants 3 bonus Horseshoe levels. With 100+ rune levels, this can reduce singularity difficulty by up to 27% — cube gain, plat cost, and hept cost effects stack for AoAG.

> ⚠ unverified: 27% max reduction cap — verify calculation against source code.
