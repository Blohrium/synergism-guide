## Content

### Half-Mind (s150)

Locks the anthill timer to 10×, making ant gains scale linearly rather than quadratically below ~2 seconds of anthill timer. Reduces the global speed debuff impact at higher singularities.

> ⚠ unverified: exact anthill timer threshold — stated as "below ~2 seconds" in PDF; verify against source code.

### One-Mind (s159–s162+)

Locks ascension speed to 10× and converts multiples of ascension speed into cube multipliers. Two effects: c15 becomes accessible without waiting for 2 seconds of in-game ascension time, and octeract gains increase (ascension speed affects oct gain, raised to the 0.5, further increased by some oct upgrades).

### Reborn Elo Grinding

Use a corruption loadout of `16/0/16/0/5/16/0/16` or a clean loadout. Use an ambrosia loadout with RCM and an obt-focused loadout. AFK through post-AoAG. Use the SLAE sheet in Re-Oct to estimate time to max the achievement.

Stats affecting ant speed and reborn elo generation: cubes, tesseracts, hypercubes, and platonic cubes opened.
