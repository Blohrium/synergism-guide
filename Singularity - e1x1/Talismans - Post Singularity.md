## Content

### Wow! Square Talisman

Must be at least Mythic before running hepteract runs. Below Mythic you miss a 20% boost.

> ⚠ unverified: Wow! Square talisman Mythic requirement — from PDF general tips; verify against v4.1+.

### Talisman Buff 1

A GQ shop upgrade. Buy when cheap — low priority but provides a meaningful talisman boost over time.

### TAEM (Talisman Ambrosia Embedding Module)

An ambrosia module unlocked post-e5x1. Include in quark and cube loadouts — it increases your Infinite Ascent rune level, which gives more quarks. See [Runes - Post Singularity](Runes%20-%20Post%20Singularity.md).
