## Beta

Max research with an offering run before opening 3D-6D cubes.

### Raised Caps

Beta raises ascension challenge caps by 5, similar to Alpha - complete these without problem, except c13 which will land around 38/40 depending on constant. Redo c15 for a bit to increase exponent.

Corruption cap raises to 13, reincarnation challenge cap raises by 10. Raise most maxed corruptions to 13, but lower Hyperchallenge to 11 to allow completions.

### p3x2 and p3x3

Similar to p3x1, Hypercube costs dominate Platonic costs for these upgrades. Open all excess Platonics once you have enough Hypercubes to cover the upgrade.

Continue 1-2 hour ascension runs to open excess Platonics and improve cubes per second. Upgrade p3x2 then p3x3 in that order - they slowly improve Platonic runs. Overnight runs are fine here.

Starting here, try raising Time Dilation to 10 and check whether you can still get a godlike Polymath quickly.

### Tributes, Hypercubes, and the c15 Push

Do your last cube run for this Singularity here. A cube run at this point produces roughly 3-10x the 3D cubes of a regular Platonic run - this ratio decreases in the next channel.

For the final c15 push, opening 5e11-1e12 Hypercubes and e23-e25 cube tributes is suggested. p3x4 eases the c15 push but is not strictly necessary for reaching 1e15 exponent.

To reach 1e15 expo: stay in your c15 run and build Obtainium (swap to Obtainium EX in shop). The two key researches are **r8x14** and **r8x19**, which both massively increase coin exponent. Manually buying talisman shards and pressing R also increases coin exponent. If you cannot reach 1e15 exponent directly, exit c15 to gain bonuses, reenter, and repeat - you will very likely reach it this way.


## 1e15 Expo - Omega

### Hepteract Forge

Once you reach 1e15 c15 expo you unlock Hepteracts, the final set of cubes and upgrades. Hepteracts cannot be used directly to buy Platonic upgrades - instead convert them into Abyss (bottom left, requires e16 c15 expo to unlock), one of 9 things forgeable from Hepteracts.

The forge has a cap. When filled, you can expand at the cost of all items in inventory - only expand when you can afford more than what you currently have.

```
Chronos:   1 + 0.6 * (hept/1000)^(1/6 + p4x4_levels/750)
Hyperreal: 1 + 0.6 * (hept/1000)^(1/3)
Quark:     (1 + 0.2 * log2(1 + hept/500))^exponent   # exponent starts at 2
Challenge: 1 + 0.5 * (hept/1000)^(1/6)
Abyss:     1.01 ^ expansions   # applies to score
```

For now, focus only on **Chronos** and **Abyss**. Goal: fill Chronos to 1000, then grind Hepteracts for 1 Abyss to buy Omega. Investing a little in Hypercube Hept and Challenge Hept is fine but slightly slower.

### Hepteract Runs and Scaling

This setup should almost always produce the maximum possible Hepteracts. Do many 1-2 hour runs to make incremental progress on score or c10 completions after each ascend.

Update campaigns to gain tokens. 1e16 expo is required to unlock Abyss Hepteract, but you will likely reach that before getting 100m Hepts. From here, cube runs are not worth it - the difference between a cube run and a Hept run is minimal.


## Omega - Singularity

You are now in the final stretch to Singularity - the longest section of this channel.

### Omega and Wow! Square

Once you get Omega, raise ascension challenge completions as high as possible, farm more Offerings for IA rune/blessings/spirits, raise c15 expo, and buy ant upgrades. Then farm Hepteracts to max every other Hepteract unlocked so far.

Defunded Education and Time Dilation lower to 11 as you can now reach e74 Obtainium, giving access to the mythic rarity **Wow! Square** talisman (only needs to be bought before you ascend). This rarity gives a free 20% odd-numbered cube bonus applied directly to the Hepteract multiplier.

### Hepteract Forge Ratios

Start leveling the first row of Hepteracts. Chronos is most important; Hyperreal is also important and better for Hypercubes, which is crucial for buying p4x2x1. Challenge Hepteracts give more c15 expo, which gives cube and ascension speed.

Maintain a Chronos:Hyperreal:Challenge ratio of around 4:2:1 or 8:4:1. Briefly use 4:4:1 or 8:8:1 as you approach p4x2x1, then revert. A multiplier of 2 in the ratio indicates one more expand.

Orbs increase Quarks gained from opening cubes, and convert to Powder after daily resets.

```
Orb:              Sig(2, o^0.5, 40) + Sig(1.5, o^0.5, 160) + Sig(1.5, o^0.5, 640) - 2
Sig(x, y, z):     1 + (x-1) * (1 - 2^(-y/z))
Powder (cubes):   min(1 + log10(p)^2/16, 1 + p/10000)
```

Spend traces of Hept income on Orbs to get slightly more cube Quarks.

Ascend every 1-2 hours and update blessings/spirits/c15 expo/Plats opened - gains compound here and this can massively shorten time to p4x2x1.

The 3 other Hepteracts in the 2nd row mainly help with Viscosity 10 runs after p4x2x1 and with c15 expo. Apart from the first 1,000, keep them at low priority until p4x2x1. Ratio between Chronos and Acc. can be 64:1 to 256:1. As a rule of thumb, expanding and filling all 3 once gives a 1.2-1.5x coin exponent.

```
Acc.:    2e6 * (hept/1000)^(1/5);  1 + 0.3 * (hept/1000)^(1/5)
Acc. B.: 1 + (hept/1000)^(1/5)
Mult.:   1e6 * (hept/1000)^(1/5);  1 + 0.3 * (hept/1000)^(1/5)
```

### p4x1 and Powder

Orbs give a bonus to Quarks gained from opening cubes, hardcapped at 200% more Quarks from cubes opened in your first Singularity.

At midnight local time, Orbs convert to Powder. Conversion ratio improves via p4x1, the Powder EX shop upgrade, and c15 expo. The initial conversion rate is poor - improve it before relying on it.

Buy at most 10 levels of Powder EX in the shop - more could hurt ascension speed and/or cube gains. p4x1 is highly needed to make Powder conversion efficient, and maxing it greatly improves the conversion ratio.

Powder gives a multiplier to cube gain, a multiplier to Quark gain, an ascension count multiplier, and a Tesseract buildings production multiplier (from p4x1).

```
Powder (cubes):  min(1 + log10(p)^2/16, 1 + p/10000)
Powder (quarks): min(1 + log10(p)/40, 1 + p/100000)
```

Consume 25 Powder to warp, which resets daily Quarks gained from cubes and ants, letting you gain them a second time. This is very good for farming Quarks to expand Quark Hepteract and get more shop upgrades.

Buy p4x1 as soon as you can without saving Hypercubes. Briefly after maxing p4x1, spend all Hepts from the next 5 hours on Orbs. Then resume normal ratios: invest 5-10% of Hepts into Orbs. Optimally, invest all Hept gains 1-2 hours before bed or midnight into Orbs, then collect 1e4-1e7 Orbs at the start of your day.

```
p4x1 cost (cubes only): [0, 0, 0, 2.5e15, 0] * 10^((l/99)^1.25)
```

### p4x2 and Viscosity

Obtained around 1e6-1e7 Chronos. The first level is a 3-7x cube multiplier - start saving Hypercubes when you reach 1e14/s. This is the only point where you should swap to Season Pass 2 rather than Season Pass 3/Y.

Raising Viscosity to 10 raises the score multiplier to the power of 3 (3.8 at max level). The other 19 levels are still strong and should be prioritised over p4x3. Since p4x2-p4x4 costs are mostly Hypercubes, you can craft Hepteracts while spending all Hypercubes on these upgrades.

At this stage you will reach the score cap at 1e23 ascension score. Any score multiplier beyond 1e23 is raised to the power of 0.5. This slightly affects the efficiency of p4x2, Challenge Hepteracts, and Abyss Hepteract expansions.

```
p4x2 cost (cubes only): [0, 0, 0, 1e19, 0] * 10^((l/19)^1.25)
```

### p4x3 and Constant

At some point between here and the end, raise Time Dilation/Defunded Education to 13 while keeping a Mythic Wow! Square talisman - this improves score and hept/s slightly. Remember to perform ant sacrifices and buy fragments occasionally. Keep Recession corruption at 11.

```
p4x3 cost (cubes only): [0, 0, 0, 1e19, 0] * 500^((l/39)^1.25)
```

### p4x4 and Chronos

p4x4 boosts your Chronos exponent, raising the 1/6 term in the Chronos formula by 1/750 per level. The effect increases with the amount of Chronos you hold. Expect somewhere between +2% and +3.5% hept/s.

```
p4x4 cost (cubes only): [0, 0, 0, 1e21, 0] * 200^((l/49)^1.25)
```

### Finale

Once p4x4 is maxed, ascend every 1-2 hours, open cubes, update blessings/spirits/c15 expo, and repeat. You could raise Time Dilation/Defunded Education from 12 to 13 here.

Around 2e12 Chronos, stop expanding Hepteracts and focus exclusively on Abyss.

p4x5 costs 1,073,741,824 Abyss, meaning you will spend roughly 2.15e17 Hepts total on Abyss. p4x5 unlocks the rune that gives access to Singularity.


## Singularity Checklist

You have acquired the Antiquities of Ant God rune and unlocked Singularity. When you Singularise, you lose all Quarks from this Singularity but gain Golden Quarks and unlock new features.

### Before You Singularise

**Quark Hepteract** - expand to 8,000, and potentially 16,000 if you can spare Quarks for permanent shop upgrades. You can sacrifice some shop upgrades like Constant EX and daily code/Powder EX levels to expand to 16,000, or half-expand to 12,000.

**Permanent Shop Upgrades** - buy permanent upgrades in the Quark shop. Buy around 250 Off/Obt potions each to push through future Singularities faster. Qhept expo is not worth it at this stage - expanding and filling Qhept to 16,000 is better than increasing the exponent.

| Upgrade | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Powder EX | Worth maxing - you will farm Orbs and Powder again at omega-singularity to warp and open cubes for Quarks |
| Constant EX | Get the 1st level, do not buy the 2nd here |
| Improved Daily Code | A few levels are fine, good for early game overall |

**Permanent Anthill** - try to obtain 1 million Anthills if you have not already. This preserves Anthills through Singularities and speeds up early game next time. Best method: set Auto Anthill Sacrifice mode to Time at 0 seconds and leave your device on overnight during a score run.

**Best Challenge 15 Exponent** - push c15 expo to its limit if you have not. Remember to buy shards and Reincarnate. c15 expo boosts Golden Quark gain from this Singularity.

**Above All Else** - the best time to Singularise is whenever you feel like it. Technically the most optimal time is ASAP every time - play at your own pace.

For the best experience, have an hour of free time before your first Singularity, and leave about 20 minutes for each Singularity afterward.

Perform your first Singularity with at least 1,000 Golden Quarks. If you don't have that much, you probably missed something - play on synergism.cc or Steam to get more Quarks if you have not already.

In addition to Shop Bonanza, Cookie Recipes I, and Starter Pack, get Cookie Recipes II if you have more than 1,000 GQ.


## Formulae

### Ascension Score Scaling

```
3D:  (score/3000)^(1/4.1)
4D:  (1 + (score - 1e5)/1e4)^0.35
5D:  (1 + (score - 1e9)/1e8)^0.5
6D:  (1 + (score - 2.666e12)/2.666e11)^0.75
7D:  (1 + (score - 1.666e16)/3.33e16)^0.85
```

Any score above 1e23 has its excess multiplier raised to the power of 0.5 (softcap).

### Cube Bank

Cube runs can gain up to 100x the 3D cubes of a score run. The 12th ant provides up to a 100% 3D cube buff. Cube banks derive from challenge completions and the 11th ant - c1-c5 provide 1 cube per challenge, c6-c10 provide 2 cubes per challenge. During cube runs, complete as many c1-c5 challenges as possible, since they dominate the challenge completions term once each is larger than 1,000.

### Platonic Upgrade Costs (Cubes only)

```
p1x1: [1e13, 1e6, 1e5, 1e4, 0] * 2^((l/299)^1.25)
p1x2: [1e11, 1e8, 1e5, 1e4, 0] * 2^((l/299)^1.25)
p1x3: [1e11, 1e6, 1e7, 1e4, 0] * 2^((l/299)^1.25)
p1x4: [1e11, 1e6, 1e5, 1e6, 0] * 2^((l/299)^1.25)
```

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>How do I reach 1e15 c15 expo?</summary>

Stay in c15 and build Obtainium (swap to Obtainium EX in shop). Get r8x14 and r8x19. Buy talisman shards and press R to increase coin exponent. If still short, exit c15 for bonuses, reenter, and repeat.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are Hepteracts and what should I focus on first?</summary>

Unlocked at 1e15 c15 expo. Focus only on Chronos and Abyss first. Fill Chronos to 1,000, then grind for 1 Abyss to buy Omega.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the Hepteract Forge and when should I expand?</summary>

The forge has a cap - when filled you can expand at the cost of all inventory. Only expand when you can afford more than what you currently have.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What do I do right before Singularity?</summary>

Expand Quark Hept to 8,000-16,000, buy permanent shop upgrades (around 250 Off/Obt potions each), get 1 million Anthills, and push c15 expo to its limit. Singularise with at least 1,000 Golden Quarks.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is Powder and how does it work?</summary>

Orbs convert to Powder at midnight local time. Powder multiplies cube gain, Quark gain, ascension count, and Tess buildings production (with p4x1). Spend 25 Powder to warp and reset daily Quark gains from cubes and ants. Improve conversion via p4x1, Powder EX shop upgrade, and c15 expo.

</details>
