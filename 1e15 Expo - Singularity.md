## beta - 1e15 Expo

Max research with an offering run before opening 3–6D cubes.

### Raised Caps

Similar to Alpha, Beta raises ascension challenge caps by 5 — complete these without problem except c13 which will be around 38/40 depending on constant. Redo c15 for a bit to increase exponent. Corruption cap raises to 13, reincarnation challenge cap raises by 10. Raise most maxed corruptions to 13 but lower Hyperchallenge corruption to 11 to allow completions.

New loadouts:
- Cube/3D: 4/13/13/13/5/13/4/11
- Score/6D: 5/13/13/13/13/13/9/10

### p3x2 and p3x3

Similar to p3x1, Hypercube costs dominate Platonic costs for these upgrades. Open all excess Platonics as long as you have enough by the time you have enough Hypercubes.

Continue doing 1–2 hour ascension runs to open excess Platonics and improve cubes per second. Gradually upgrade p3x2 then p3x3 in that order — they slowly improve Platonic runs. Overnight runs are fine here.

Starting here, try raising Time Dilation to 10 and check whether you can get godlike Polymath quickly.

### Tributes, Hypercubes, and c15 Double Spirit Climb

Do your last cube run for this Singularity here. A cube run at this point produces about 3–10x 3D cubes compared to a regular Platonic run, and this ratio decreases in the next channel.

For the final c15 push, opening 5e11–1e12 Hypercubes and e23–e25 cube tributes is suggested.

p3x4 eases the c15 push but isn't strictly necessary for reaching 1e15 exponent.

To reach 1e15 expo: stay in your c15 run and build up Obtainium (swap to Obtainium EX in shop). The 2 important researches are r8x14 and r8x19, both of which massively increase coin exponent. Manually buying talisman shards and pressing R also increases coin exponent. If unable to reach 1e15 exponent directly, exit c15 to gain massive bonuses, reenter, repeat. You will very likely reach e15 exponent this way.

## 1e15 Expo - Omega

### Hepteract and Hepteract Forge

Once you reach 1e15 c15 expo you unlock Hepteracts, the final sets of cubes and upgrades. Hepteracts cannot be used directly to buy Platonic upgrades — instead convert them into Abyss (bottom left, requires e16 c15 expo to unlock), one of 9 things you can forge from Hepteracts.

The Hepteract Forge has a cap. When filled, you can expand at the cost of all items in inventory. Always expand only when you can afford more than what you currently have.

There are 9 upgrades including Orbs:
- **Chronos** — +60% linear to 1000, formula: (1+0.6*(chronos/1000)^(1/6)). p4x4 raises this exponent.
- **Quark Hept** — buy only when you have excess quarks.
- **Abyss** — currency, doesn't do anything on its own. With Tier 14 Ascension Score achievement, Abyss also provides a 1% score buff every expansion.

For now focus only on Chronos and Abyss. Goal: fill Chronos to 1000, then grind Hepteracts for 1 Abyss to buy Omega. Investing a bit in Hypercube Hept and Challenge Hept is fine but slightly slower.

### Hepteract Runs and c15 Scaling

Main corruption loadout for this section: **6/13/13/13/13/13/11/11**

This should almost always produce the maximum possible Hepteracts. Do many 1–2 hour runs to make incremental progress on score or c10 completions after each ascend.

Update campaigns to gain tokens. 1e16 expo is required to unlock Abyss Hepteract but you will likely reach that before getting 100m hepts. From now on, cube runs are not worth it as the difference between a cube run and a hept run is minimal.

## Omega - Singularity

Congratulations — you are in the final stretch to Singularity, the longest section.

### Omega and Wow! Square

Once you get Omega, raise ascension challenge completions as high as possible, farm more Offerings for IA rune, blessings, spirits, raise c15 expo, buy ant upgrades. Then farm Hepteracts to max every other Hepteract in the forge unlocked so far.

Corruption setup: **6/13/13/13/11/13/11/7**

DE and TD are lowered to 11 as you can now reach e74 Obtainium, giving access to the mythic rarity Wow! Square talisman (only needs to be bought before you ascend). This rarity gives a free 20% odd numbered cube bonus applied directly to the Hepteract multiplier.

### Hepteract Forge Ratios

Start leveling the first row of Hepteracts. Chronos is most important; Hyperreal is also important and better for Hypercubes, which is crucial for buying p4x2x1. Challenge Hepteracts give more c15 expo, which gives cube and ascension speed.

Maintain a Chronos:Hyperreal:Challenge ratio of around 4:2:1 or 8:4:1. Briefly use 4:4:1 or 8:8:1 as you approach p4x2x1, then revert. A multiplier of 2 in the ratio indicates one more expand.

Orbs increase Quarks gained from opening cubes. Orbs convert to Powder after daily resets. Powder boosts cubes: min(1+log10(p)^2/16, 1+p/10000). Spend traces of Hept income on Orbs to get slightly more cube Quarks.

Ascend every 1–2 hours and update blessings/spirits/c15 expo/Plats opened — gains are compounding and this can massively shorten time to p4x2x1.

The 3 other Hepteracts in the 2nd row mainly help with Viscosity 10 runs after p4x2x1 and c15 expo. Apart from the first 1000, keep them at low priority until p4x2x1. Ratio between Chronos and accelerator can be 64:1 to 256:1. Rule of thumb: expanding and filling all 3 once gives a 1.2–1.5x coin exponent.

### p4x1 and Powder

Orbs give a bonus to Quarks gained from opening cubes, with a hardcap of 200% more Quarks from cubes opened in your first Singularity.

At midnight local time, Orbs convert to Powder. Conversion ratio improves via: p4x1, Powder EX shop upgrade, and c15 expo. Initial conversion rate is poor — improve it before relying on it.

Buy at most 10 levels of Powder EX in the shop — more could hurt ascension speed and/or cube gains. p4x1 is highly needed to make Powder conversion efficient. Max p4x1 greatly improves the conversion ratio.

Powder gives: multiplier to cube gain, multiplier to Quark gain, ascension count multiplier, and Tesseract buildings production multiplier (from p4x1). Consume 25 Powder to warp, which resets daily Quarks gained from cubes and ants, allowing you to gain them a second time. Very good for farming Quarks to expand Quark Hepteract and get more shop upgrades.

Buy p4x1 as soon as you can without saving Hypercubes. Briefly after maxing p4x1, spend all Hepts from the next 5 hours on Orbs. Then resume normal ratios: invest 5–10% of Hepts into Orbs. Optimally invest all Hept gains 1–2 hours before bed/midnight into Orbs, then get 1e4–1e7 Orbs at the start of your day.

### p4x2 and Viscosity

Obtained around 1e6–1e7 Chronos. The first level is a 3–7x cube multiplier — start saving Hypercubes when you reach 1e14/s. This is the only time you should swap to Season Pass 2 rather than Season Pass 3/Y.

New corruption loadout for the rest of this channel: **10/13/13/13/12/11/12/9**

Raising Viscosity to 10 raises the score multiplier to ^3 (3.8 at max level). The other 19 levels are still strong and should be prioritised over p4x3. As p4x2–p4x4 costs are mostly Hypercubes, you can craft Hepteracts while spending all Hypercubes on these upgrades.

At this stage you will reach the score cap at 1e23 ascension score. Any multiplier beyond 1e23 is raised to the 0.5. This slightly affects efficiency of p4x2, Challenge Hepteracts, and Abyss Hepteract expansions.

### p4x3 and Constant

At some point between here and the end, raise TD/DE to 13 while keeping Mythic Wow! Square, improving score and hept/s slightly. Remember to perform ant sacrifices and buy fragments occasionally. Keep Recession corruption at 11.

### p4x4 and Chronos

p4x4 boosts your Chronos exponent, raising the 1/6 in (1+0.6*(chronos/1000)^(1/6)) by 1/750 per level. Effect increases with the amount of Chronos. Expect somewhere between +2 and +3.5% hept/s.

### Finale

Once p4x4 is maxed, ascend every 1–2 hours, open cubes, update blessings/spirits/c15 expo, repeat. You could raise Time Dilation/Defunded Education from 12 to 13.

Around 2e12 Chronos, stop expanding Hepteracts and focus exclusively on Abyss.

p4x5 costs 1,073,741,824 Abyss, meaning you will spend a total of ~2.15e17 Hepts on Abyss. p4x5 unlocks a rune that gives you access to Singularity.

## Singularity Checklist

### I have acquired the Antiquities of Ant God Rune. Now what?

You have unlocked Singularity (Sing). When you Singularise, you lose all Quarks from this Singularity but gain Golden Quarks and unlock new features.

### Things to do before you Singularise

**Quark Hepteract**
Expand Quark Hepteract to 8000, and potentially 16000 if you can spare Quarks for permanent shop upgrades. You can sacrifice some shop upgrades like Constant EX and daily code/powder EX levels to expand to 16000 or half-expand to 12000.

**Permanent Shop Upgrades**
Buy permanent upgrades in the Quark shop. Buy around 250 Off/Obt potions each to push through next Singularities faster. Qhept expo is not worth it at this stage — expanding and filling Qhept to 16000 is better than increasing the exponent.

- **Powder EX** — worth maxing. Once back at omega-singularity you will be farming Orbs and Powder again to warp and open cubes for Quarks.
- **Constant EX** — get the 1st level, don't buy the 2nd here.
- **Improved Daily Code** — a few levels are fine, good for early game overall.

**Permanent Anthill**
Try to obtain 1 million Anthills if you haven't already. This preserves Anthills through Singularities and speeds up early game next time. Best method: set auto Anthill sacrifice mode to Time at 0 seconds and leave device on overnight during a score run.

**Best Challenge 15 Exponent**
Push c15 expo to its limit if you haven't. Remember to buy shards and Reincarnate. c15 expo boosts Golden Quark gain from this Singularity.

**Above All Else**
The best time to Singularise is whenever you feel like it. Technically the most optimal time is ASAP every time. Play at your own pace.

For the best experience, have an hour of free time before your first Singularity. Leave about 20 minutes for each Singularity afterwards.

Perform your first Singularity with at least 1000 Golden Quarks. If you don't have it, you probably missed something. Play on synergism.cc or Steam to get more Quarks if you haven't already.

In addition to Shop Bonanza, Cookie Recipes I, and Starter Pack, get Cookie Recipes II if you have more than 1000 GQ.

## Formulae

### Score Scaling
The bonus to different cubes from Ascension score:
- 3D: (score/3000)^(1/4.1)
- 4D: [1+(score-1e5)/1e4]^(0.35)
- 5D: [1+(score-1e9)/1e8]^(0.5)
- 6D: [1+(score-2.666e12)/2.666e11]^(0.75)
- 7D: [1+(score-1.666e16)/3.33e16]^(0.85)

Extra softcap for scores above 1e23 — any multiple of score above 1e23 is raised to 0.5.

### Cube Bank
Cube runs can gain up to 100x the 3D cubes vs a score run. The 12th ant provides up to a 100% 3D cube buff. Cube banks are derived from challenge completions and the 11th ant. c1–5 provides 1 cube per challenge, c6–10 provides 2 cubes per challenge. During cube runs, complete as many c1–5 challenges as possible as they dominate the challenge completions term when larger than 1000 each.

### Hepteract Forge Formulae
- Chronos: (1+0.6*(hept/1000)^(1/6+1/750*p4x4))
- Hyperreal: (1+0.6*(hept/1000)^(1/3))
- Quark: (1+0.2*log2(1+hept/500))^(exponent, initially 2)
- Challenge: (1+0.5*(hept/1000)^(1/6))
- Abyss: 1.01^(expansions) on score
- Acc.: 2e6*(hept/1000)^(1/5); (1+0.3*(hept/1000)^(1/5))
- Acc. B.: (1+(hept/1000)^(1/5))
- Mult.: 1e6*(hept/1000)^(1/5); (1+0.3*(hept/1000)^(1/5))
- Orb: Sig(2, o^0.5, 40) + Sig(1.5, o^0.5, 160) + Sig(1.5, o^0.5, 640) - 2, where Sig(x,y,z): 1+(x-1)*(1-(2^(-y/z)))
- Powder (cubes): min(1+log10(p)^2/16, 1+p/10000)
- Powder (quarks): min(1+log10(p)/40, 1+p/100000)

### Platonic Upgrade Costs (Cubes only)
- p1x1: [1e13, 1e6, 1e5, 1e4, 0]*2^[(l/299)^1.25]
- p1x2: [1e11, 1e8, 1e5, 1e4, 0]*2^[(l/299)^1.25]
- p1x3: [1e11, 1e6, 1e7, 1e4, 0]*2^[(l/299)^1.25]
- p1x4: [1e11, 1e6, 1e5, 1e6, 0]*2^[(l/299)^1.25]
- p4x1: [0, 0, 0, 2.5e15, 0]*10^[(l/99)^1.25]
- p4x2: [0, 0, 0, 1e19, 0]*10^[(l/19)^1.25]
- p4x3: [0, 0, 0, 1e19, 0]*500^[(l/39)^1.25]
- p4x4: [0, 0, 0, 1e21, 0]*200^[(l/49)^1.25]
