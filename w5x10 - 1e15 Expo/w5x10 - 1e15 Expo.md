## Challenge 15

After completing c14 you unlock c15. c15 is different — you can never complete it, but you gain buffs from the highest coin exponent reached in c15 (Wow! Cubes > Challenge 15). Immediately enter c15, wait a handful of seconds, leave, and you gain buffs. c15 expo updates based on the coin amount when you leave, not the highest of the run.

After this, retry some ascension challenges, especially c14, as multiple c14 completions become easy after the first.

Do a c15 run every 1–2 score runs from now on.

Secret c15 strategy: turn off auto research, use an obt potion, then buy r1x21 and r2x11x9.

## Run Cycle

Once you reach this channel, follow this run cycle for the rest of this guide:

**Cube → Obt/Off → c15**

Spend most time on score runs, doing the others for a short time (1–3 minutes). A score run should never be longer than 24 hours.

## Platonic Cubes

You unlock a new cube type called Platonics and corresponding upgrades. You won't yet have enough score to reach them.

## Research and r8x25

At some point here or before, you unlock Tesseract building automation. Activate it, set to Percentage mode at 30. Spend all tess in buildings for now, raising the percentage later. After activating, Tesseract buildings are automatically allocated and purchased after every ascension regardless of whether you completed c10.

r8x25 is a crucial permanent research between you and w5x10. Requirements: at least 80b tributes, 100m gifts, and 750k benedictions.

Swap shop upgrades to Obtainium EX to speed up the process and avoid sacrificing ants, as Obtainium gain depends on ant sac amount per w5x7.

## Platonics and Maximising Cubes

You will start gaining some Platonics but won't gain much more than 10–20 per ascension second, so they don't matter yet.

To buy w5x10, maximise cube gain by lowering corruption to allow more challenge completions without losing too much score. Useful loadouts at this stage:
- 1/5/4/11/1/11/4/3
- 1/10/4/11/1/11/4/7
- 3/11/4/11/4/11/4/7 for higher score

Continue doing 1–2 hour ascensions to open cubes/hypercubes and improve ascension score.

Once you have 1e14 tributes, start buying w5x10 — spend 50% cubes on w5x10 and 50% on tributes. Once you reach 2e16 tributes, spend all cubes on w5x10 until maxed. w5x10 costs 5e15 cubes total.

## w5x10 - alpha

### Platonic Cubes and Platonic Upgrades

After maxing w5x10, main goals are opening 12 million Platonic cubes and buying Alpha. You can buy up to 100 p1x1 and 50 p1x2. Ignore p1x3 and p1x4 in this channel. Save Plats for Alpha once you hit the 12 million softcap.

### Polymath, Time Dilation, and Ascension Speed

The Polymath talisman gives an Ascension speed bonus depending on rarity. Target godlike rarity at level 360 (+32.5% ascension speed), though extraordinary at level 180 also gives +30%. Lower Time Dilation and/or Defunded Education until you can acquire Polymath from saccing ants. Buy it quickly as the ascension time multiplier stacks in real time. If unable to acquire your intended Polymath talisman within 10 minutes, lower Time Dilation.

Corruption loadout for this section: **3/11/4/11/11/11/4-5/11**

Continue adjusting Time Dilation in following sections to maximise score while maintaining godlike Polymath talisman.

## alpha - p2x1x10

### Alpha

Alpha allows 5 extra ascension challenge completions each, which you should complete immediately. Then enter c15 for extra exponent. Buy the 6th rune, Infinite Ascent, which gives +0.2% quarks and +1% cubes per level.

New corruption loadouts due to raised cap from 11 to 12. TD can be 5 or 6 depending on stats:
- Cube/3D: 1/12/4/12/4/12/4/11
- Score/6D: 3/12/4/12/12/12/5/9

Prioritise maxing p1x1 and p1x2 before moving on.

### p1x3 and Hypercube Bottlenecks

Prioritise Platonics toward maxing p1x3 if not yet maxed. Score scaling explanation: as you gain more score, Hypercubes grow slower than Platonics. Platonics scale at ^0.75 while Hypercubes scale at ^0.5 relative to score. From here, start being aware of when to save Hypercubes for Platonic upgrades.

### Platonics Distribution

If you haven't opened 12.1 million Platonics, do that first. Then buy at least 50 p1x4 levels, then split Platonics evenly between p1x4 and p2x1.

### p2x1 and Viscosity

Maxing p2x1 is the goal of this section. Buy levels one at a time — they provide a strong buff allowing more challenge completions and raising Viscosity. Suggested breakpoints:
- 3/12/4/12/12/12/6/9 at p2x1x2
- 4/12/4/12/12/12/6/9 at p2x1x5
- 5/12/4/12/12/12/6/11 at p2x1x8

### Secret Achievements

If missing any secret achievements, try to obtain all of them before moving on. Secret achievements provide AP that compounds into significant progress over the Singularity. See the secret achievements reference for the full list.

## p2x1x10 - p3x1 - Beta

Max research with an offering run before opening 3–6D cubes.

### p2x2, p2x3 and p2x4

Purchase order p2x4 → p2x3 → p2x2 or p2x3 → p2x4 → p2x2 — difference is minimal as this section is short. After p2x2 you can raise TD as long as you maintain godlike Polymath. By the time you max p2x2 your loadout will be 5/12/4/12/12/12/8/11.

### p3x1 and Inflation

p3x1 essentially lets you bypass the Deflation corruption by mirroring particles to diamond, allowing you to raise Deflation directly to maximum.

Loadout after p3x1: **5/12/12/12/12/12/8/11**

Be very aware of Hypercube amounts here as most will be spent on Platonic upgrades. Raise the percentage saved in auto Tesseract buildings to around 70% to open some Tesseracts.

### Beta

Beta has lower priority than p3x1 due to high cost. Ascend occasionally, open cube/tess, update spirits/blessings/IA rune, update c15 expo, repeat. Keep sufficient Hypercubes for the upgrade.
